# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We aim to maintain a respectful and constructive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- respectful and professional communication
- discussion focused on code/problems (not people)
- evidence-based feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, hate, discrimination, personal attacks
- threats or privacy abuse
- repeated disruption after maintainer notice

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove/reject violating content and restrict participation when necessary.

## Reporting

Send incident details (links/screenshots) to `i_am@curogom.dev`.
